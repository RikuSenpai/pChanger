Do NOT change the name of the folder unless you changed the code!
If you change the name without changing the code, the models will NOT work!

1. Navigate to "...\Steam\steamapps\common\Counter-Strike Global Offensive\csgo\models"
2. Move the "pChanger" folder into the directory above
3. Start Counter-Strike: Global Offensive
4. Inject pChanger
5. Open the GUI
6. Open Misc Tab
7. Open Page #2
8. Click on "Enable Custom Models"
9. Select Preferred Models
10. Click "Update"
